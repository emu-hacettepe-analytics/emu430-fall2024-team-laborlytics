How does the game work?

The game gives points based on three indicators. Lower unemployment is better, while higher employment and higher labor force participation are better.

A total score of 8 or 9 means Strong, 5 to 7 means Moderate, and 3 or 4 means Weak.

This tool uses all monthly observations from the cleaned CSV data set.
